About TEDxSabonGari

Ideas worth spreading in our community and beyond

Introducing TEDxSabonGari

Welcome to TEDxSabonGari, a proudly licensed local TEDx event committed to spreading powerful ideas and inspiring change in our community and beyond.

What is TEDx?

In the spirit of TED’s mission, “ideas worth spreading,” TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These events are fully planned and coordinated independently, under a free license granted by TED.

TEDxSabonGari: Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To build a dynamic, informed, and inspired local community where great ideas lead to real impact.

Mission

To create a platform where diverse voices and brilliant minds from Sabon Gari and beyond can connect, inspire, and drive meaningful change.

What Makes TEDxSabonGari Unique?

TEDxSabonGari is not just an event, it's a movement. Rooted in the rich cultural and intellectual diversity of Sabon Gari Local Government and beyond, our goal is to showcase stories, innovations, and ideas that reflect the aspirations of our people, especially the youth.

Through TEDxSabonGari, we aim to:

  • Provide a stage for local thinkers, creators, professionals, and dreamers.
  • Connect our community to global conversations.
  • Inspire solutions to local and global challenges.

Theme: "Bridging the Gap"

Our theme will explore ideas around social, economic, cultural, and technological divides and how innovation, collaboration, and empathy can help us close those gaps.

We’ll feature inspiring talks, performances, exhibitions, and networking opportunities, bringing together professionals, youth, and thought leaders.

Join us as we celebrate ideas that can transform Sabon Gari and the world.
