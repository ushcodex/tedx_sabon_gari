What is TEDxSabonGari ?

TEDxSabonGari is not just an event, it's a movement. Rooted in the rich cultural and intellectual diversity of Sabon Gari Local Government and beyond, our goal is to showcase stories, innovations, and ideas that reflect the aspirations of our people, especially the youth.

    Through TEDxSabonGari, we aim to:

  • Provide a stage for local thinkers, creators, professionals, and dreamers.
  • Connect our community to global conversations.
  • Inspire solutions to local and global challenges.
