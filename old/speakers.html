Our Distinguished Speakers

Meet the thought leaders, innovators, and changemakers who will share powerful ideas at TEDxSabonGari 2026

Dr. Abubakar Siddique Mohammed

Director, Centre for Democratic Development Research and Training (CEDDERT)

A senior lecturer at Ahmadu Bello University and director of CEDDERT, Dr. Mohammed is a leading voice on democratic governance, public policy, and development in Nigeria. With advanced degrees from French institutions, he brings scholarly insight to critical issues of human rights, governance reforms, and youth development.

Dr. Zaid Abubakar

Executive Chairman, Kano State Internal Revenue Service

A transformational tax leader and award-winning accountant driving fiscal governance reforms in Nigeria. As Executive Chairman of Kano State IRS, he's leading digitization efforts and taxpayer engagement. A member of Nigeria's Presidential Fiscal Policy and Tax Reforms Committee, he holds certifications in Data Science and AI from MIT.

Prof. Abdulhamidu Abdullahi

Deputy Coordinator, NACTAL Kaduna State

A seasoned academia and community development worker focused on advocacy and human development. He serves in leadership roles with NACTAL and the Kaduna State Local Government Accountability Mechanism. A regular public commentator on radio and TV, he addresses community development, youth empowerment, and conflict management.

Engr. Dr. A. S. Abdurrasheed

Lecturer, Ahmadu Bello University Zaria

An award-winning researcher and innovator passionate about sustainable engineering solutions and youth empowerment. With a Ph.D. in Civil & Environmental Engineering, he has authored over 28 publications and secured multiple research grants. Founder of the ABU Energy Innovation Challenge, aiming to transition universities to renewable energy.

Hajiya Adama Haruna Abdulmalik

Northern Regional Coordinator, AJoke Ayisat Afolabi Foundation

A dynamic development practitioner and gender advocate dedicated to social justice, women's inclusion, and grassroots transformation in Northern Nigeria. With experience in both government and NGO sectors, she champions the rights of marginalized communities and has led impactful initiatives in education, healthcare, and women empowerment.

Abdulhaleem Ishaq Ringim

Special Assistant to Governor of Kaduna State on Economic Matters

An award-winning writer and economic policy enthusiast influencing governance and development discourse in Nigeria. At 24, he became the youngest participant in Nigeria's premier policy think tank program. Currently serves as Kaduna State Coordinator for AUDA-NEPAD and has conceptualized the Climate Action Index for evaluating subnational climate policies.

Hon. Hafsat Aminu Adhama

Senior Special Assistant to Governor of Kano State on Girl Child Education

A trailblazer in education reform and girls' empowerment who has enrolled over 3,000 out-of-school girls and mentored 10,000+ across Kano State. Leads the World Bank-funded AGILE Second Chance Education Project and serves as Chief Visionary Officer of ESAHUB Africa, empowering youth with entrepreneurial skills.

Khalil Kabir Mantissa, Esq.

Assistant Secretary, Nigerian Bar Association Zaria Branch

A resilient legal practitioner with an inspiring story of self-determination. Graduated with a 2:1 from ABU Zaria and Nigerian Law School, now leading his own law firm with a 90% case success rate. A former sought-after MC and columnist, he overcame financial challenges by working multiple jobs to fund his education.

Dr. Ahmad Bello

Resident Doctor, Ahmadu Bello University Teaching Hospital

A dedicated physician and public health leader with expertise in global health, epidemiology, and outbreak response. Holds over 15 international certifications and recently earned a Master's in Public Health. Serves as Chairman of the Chikaji Unity and Development Association, bringing clinical expertise to underserved populations in Kaduna State.

Abdulmuqeet Hussain

CEO, BT Hub

A trailblazing digital leader who has trained over 50,000 individuals in digital skills and innovation. Through partnerships with tech giants like Microsoft and Google, he's creating waves of transformation in education, business, and technology across Nigeria. His work focuses on Data Science, AI, STEM Education, and bridging the digital divide.

Hon. Zubairu Mukhtar

Special Assistant to Deputy Governor of Kaduna State

A dynamic public administrator and policy strategist with over a decade of experience in Nigeria's public service. Currently a Ph.D. student in Public Administration at ABU Zaria, he bridges government, communities, and development partners. Founder of the Gamji Youth & Women Empowerment Initiative, a self-funded NGO empowering communities across Kaduna.

Salim Yunusa

Founder, Poetic Wednesdays Initiative

A creative communications strategist and literary curator promoting creative expression in Northern Nigeria. Founder of the pioneering Kano International Poetry Festival and a freelance journalist with HumAngle Media. With a background in Conflict Management and Peace Studies, he integrates spatial justice and peacebuilding into his literary and advocacy work.

Alhaji Sunusi Abdullahi

founder and Managing Director of SAJ Foods, makers of the popular SAN Youghut.

‎ ‎Alhaji Sunusi began with a simple but bold idea: produce wholesome, affordable dairy products that meet local tastes and standards. Through perseverance, quality focus, and community-minded leadership, he transformed that idea into SAJ Foods a Zaria-based dairy company now known across Northern Nigeria for its yoghurt, dairy beverages and growing product range. His journey from small outlet to industrial producer is both practical and inspiring, showing how local value-creation can meet national needs. ‎ Please welcome to the TEDxSabonGari stage Alhaji Sunusi Abdullahi

Dr Fahad Chikaji Ahmad

Doctor of Excellence, Kaduna State Gender Ambassador, and renowned Tax Advocate

Dr. Fahad brings a wealth of knowledge, experience, and transformative insight. With a solid foundation in Information Management and Computer Science, he is a respected member of top professional bodies including the Nigerian Computer Society (MNCS), the Computer Professionals Registration Council of Nigeria (MCPN), and the Institute of Certified Public Accountants (MICPA). ‎ ‎Dr. Fahad is a passionate voice for inclusive governance, digital innovation, and youth empowerment. His leadership is rooted in integrity, knowledge, and a drive to see sustainable change across systems and societies. ‎ ‎At TEDxSabonGari 2026, he will be sharing powerful ideas shaped by experience, vision, and action.

Saratu Samaila

a powerful voice for inclusivity, youth empowerment, and sustainable development

‎ ‎Saratu is a passionate advocate and changemaker whose life and work are deeply rooted in the belief that inclusivity is a right, not a privilege. A young woman with a visual disability (blindness), she has transformed personal experience into purpose, dedicating her journey to amplifying the voices of youths and marginalized communities across Nigeria. She is the Founder of the Muryar Matasa Initiative for Employment, Development, and Inclusion, a youth-led organization committed to promoting sustainable development, job creation, and equal opportunities for all. ‎ ‎ ‎At TEDxSabonGari, Saratu Samaila represents resilience, vision, and the transformative power of inclusive leadership. Her story reminds us that when barriers are removed, potential thrives and society moves forward together.
